DOMAINSBLACKLISTS

Building a service on top of a regularly updated blacklists can provide immense value, not only for individual internet users but also for businesses and cybersecurity professionals. Whatever service you choose to build, ensure it's user-friendly, reliable, and secure.

Downloads

Features

  • ✔️ Hourly Updates: stay protected against emerging threats
  • ✔️ Comprehensive Coverage: aggregated from the most frequently updated blacklists
  • ✔️ Broad Compatibility: works across browsers, firewalls, proxies, and more
  • ✔️ Robust Security: protect against phishing, spam, scams, ads, trackers, bad websites and more
  • ✔️ Whitelist Capability: submit domain for whitelisting
  • ✔️ Local Mirror: set up easily using the Docker image
